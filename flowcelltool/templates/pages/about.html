About Flowcelltool

Flowcelltool is a small application for the visual management of flow cell sample sheets for Illumina sequencing machines. The tool is developed at the Core Unit for Bioinformatics of the Berlin Institute of Health.

The software is provided as open source software in the hope that it is useful but comes without any warranty (see below).
